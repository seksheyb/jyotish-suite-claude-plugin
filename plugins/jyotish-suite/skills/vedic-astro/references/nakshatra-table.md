## Pada Calculation

Each Nakshatra has 4 Padas of 3°20' each.

To find Pada: take the planet's degrees within its Nakshatra, divide by 3°20'.
- 0°00'–3°19' = Pada 1
- 3°20'–6°39' = Pada 2
- 6°40'–9°59' = Pada 3
- 10°00'–13°19' = Pada 4

**Pada signs cycle from Aries:**
Each Nakshatra's Padas map to Aries, Taurus, Gemini, Cancer in sequence.
Then the next Nakshatra continues: Leo, Virgo, Libra, Scorpio, etc.
The full 108-Pada cycle goes through all 12 signs 9 times.

## Gandanta Zones (Critical Degree Ranges)

Gandanta = knot between water and fire signs. Deeply karmic.

| Zone | D1 Range |
|------|----------|
| Cancer→Leo | 26°40' Cancer to 3°20' Leo (Ashlesha 4 → Magha 1) |
| Scorpio→Sagittarius | 26°40' Scorpio to 3°20' Sagittarius (Jyeshtha 4 → Mula 1) |
| Pisces→Aries | 26°40' Pisces to 3°20' Aries (Revati 4 → Ashwini 1) |

Planets within these ranges carry unresolved karmic knots. The themes of the planet's houses require conscious effort to untangle in this lifetime.
