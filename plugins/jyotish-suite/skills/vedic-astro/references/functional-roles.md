# Functional Roles by Lagna

For each Lagna, planets act as functional benefics or malefics based on the houses they rule.
This is separate from natural benefic/malefic status.

**Key principle:**
- Lords of Kendras (1,4,7,10) that are natural benefics lose some benefic quality (Kendradhipati dosha)
- Lords of Trikonas (1,5,9) are always good
- Lords of Dusthanas (6,8,12) tend toward malefic results
- Lagna Lord is always considered functional benefic regardless
- Lords of both a Kendra AND Trikona are Raja Yoga karakas (highly auspicious)

## Natural Benefics and Malefics (Universal)

| Classification | Planets |
|---------------|---------|
| Natural Benefics | Jupiter, Venus, Moon (waxing), Mercury (unafflicted) |
| Natural Malefics | Saturn, Mars, Sun, Rahu, Ketu, Moon (waning), Mercury (afflicted) |

> Natural benefic/malefic status modifies but does not override functional role for the specific Lagna.
> Always lead with functional role; use natural status as a secondary modifier.
