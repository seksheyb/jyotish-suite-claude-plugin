# Vedic Astrology Reading Methodology

Full systematic framework for every reading. Execute every step in sequence.

---

## Step 0 — Chart Baseline *(Run Once Per Session)*

Before any question-specific analysis, establish:

- **Lagna and Lagna Lord** — sign, degree, Nakshatra, Pada, house placement of Lagna Lord
- **Functional Benefics and Malefics** — classify every planet by its lordship for this Lagna (see `functional-roles.md`)
- **Atmakaraka and Amatyakaraka** — highest and second-highest degree planets in D1
- **Vargottama planets** — same sign in D1 and D9; flag as significantly strengthened
- **Combust planets** — within combustion range of Sun; flag as suppressed
  - Sun combustion orbs: Moon 12°, Mars 17°, Mercury 14° (superior), Jupiter 11°, Venus 10°, Saturn 15°
- **Current Dasha / Antardasha / Pratyantar** — compute from Vimshottari Dasha using Moon's Nakshatra at birth
- **Full Degree Flag Scan** — for every planet:
  - Gandanta? (within 3°20' of Cancer/Leo, Scorpio/Sagittarius, Pisces/Aries junctions)
  - Mrityu Bhaga? (check `degree-flags.md` for sign-specific death degrees)
  - Pushkara Navamsa or Pushkara Bhaga? (check `degree-flags.md`)
  - Sandhi? (0°00'–0°59' = immature; 29°00'–29°59' = unstable)
  - Planetary War? (within 1° of another planet — lower degree wins)
- **Full Aspect Map** — pre-map all planet-to-planet aspects:
  - Which planets aspect each other (one-way vs mutual)
  - Orb of each aspect (tight = within 3°)
  - Flag mutual aspects — treat as quasi-conjunctions

## Step 2 — D1 House Analysis *(Repeat for each relevant house)*

### a. House Nature
- Sign on cusp → element (Fire/Earth/Air/Water), modality (Cardinal/Fixed/Mutable)
- Classification: Kendra (1,4,7,10) / Trikona (1,5,9) / Dusthana (6,8,12) / Upachaya (3,6,10,11)
- Natural significations of the house

### b. Planets in the House

For each planet:
- Sign, degree, Nakshatra, Pada
- **Degree checks:** Gandanta / Mrityu Bhaga / Pushkara / Sandhi / Planetary War
- **Nakshatra Lord** — which planet rules this Nakshatra? What houses does it rule for this Lagna? What does it signify?
- **Pada Lord** — the Pada Lord is the lord of the planet's D9 sign (Navamsa sign). The D9 is always derived in the baseline; look up the planet's D9 sign there, then identify the lord of that sign. That lord is the Pada Lord. What houses does it rule in D1? How does its nature and placement add texture to how this planet expresses?
- **Nakshatra Gana:**
  - Deva Gana = structured, righteous, consistent expression
  - Manushya Gana = conditional, worldly, self-interested expression
  - Rakshasa Gana = intense, unconventional, transformative expression
- **Dignity:**
  - Exalted / Own Sign / Moolatrikona / Friendly / Neutral / Enemy / Debilitated
  - Neecha Bhanga (debilitation cancellation) — check if lord of debilitation sign is in Kendra from Lagna or Moon
- **Combustion** — within Sun's orb? Flag as suppressed
- **Functional role** — benefic or malefic for this Lagna?

### c. House Lord
- Where is the House Lord placed? House, sign, degree, Nakshatra, Pada
- Full degree checks (Gandanta, Mrityu Bhaga, Pushkara, Sandhi, War)
- Nakshatra Lord and Pada Lord analysis *(use the D9 sign of the house lord to identify its Pada Lord)*
- Dignity and combustion check
- **House connection** — the house the lord sits in is connected to the house it rules (mutual activation)

### d. Aspects Received by the House *(from planets)*

For each aspecting planet:
- Which planet, from which house, by which aspect type:
  - All planets: 7th aspect (opposition)
  - Mars: 4th and 8th aspects (additional)
  - Jupiter: 5th and 9th aspects (additional)
  - Saturn: 3rd and 10th aspects (additional)
  - Rahu/Ketu: 5th and 9th aspects (used by many schools — apply if following this convention)
- **Orb to house cusp** — tight (within 3°) = powerful; moderate (3°–7°); loose (7°+) = background
- **Dignity of aspecting planet** — exalted aspect uplifts; debilitated aspect afflicts
- **Degree flag of aspecting planet** — Gandanta or Mrityu Bhaga planet carries its affliction into the aspected house
- **Functional role** — benefic or malefic for this Lagna?
- One-way or mutual aspect? Note if the house lord also aspects back

### e. Ashtakavarga
- House score: below 25 = weak / 25–30 = average / above 30 = strong
- House lord's placement house score

### f. Planet-to-Planet Aspects *(Critical — Separate Layer)*

For each planetary aspect relevant to the question:

**Identify the pair:**
- Which two planets are in aspect and by what angle?
- One-way or mutual?

**One-way aspect:**
- Planet A aspects Planet B — A's energy colors how B expresses
- B receives A's significations, lordships, and quality

**Mutual aspect (quasi-conjunction):**
- Both planets aspect each other
- Treat as energetically fused — their significations, lordships, natures BLEND
- Neither can be read in full isolation for this chart
- Friendly mutual aspects = cooperative fusion; enemy mutual aspects = internal conflict

**Orb:**
- Within 3° = behaves like conjunction in intensity
- 3°–7° = moderate influence
- Beyond 7° = loose background connection

**Nature of exchange:**
- Natural friendship/enmity between the two planets
- Functional role of each for this Lagna (both benefic / both malefic / mixed)
- Dignity differential — more dignified planet tends to dominate

**Degree flags:**
- If either planet is at Mrityu Bhaga, Gandanta, or Sandhi — that weakness infects the aspect

**Net effect:**
- Does this aspect support, complicate, or block the question's theme?
- What lordship themes are fused? (e.g., lords of 1st and 9th in mutual aspect = self and fortune linked)

## Step 3 — D9 (Navamsa) Analysis *(Repeat for each relevant house)*

Run the complete Step 2 sequence (a through f) within D9:

- **House nature in D9** — same sign analysis
- **Planets in D9 house** — sign, degree (approximate), dignity in D9
- **D9 house lord** — placement and dignity
- **Aspects on D9 house** — orb check applies
- **Planet-to-planet aspects in D9** — repeat Step 2f within D9
- **Degree checks within D9** — Gandanta, Mrityu Bhaga, Pushkara, Sandhi still apply
- **Vargottama flag** — planet in same sign in D1 and D9 = D1 promise amplified into soul level

**D9 interpretation rule:**
- Strong D9 = D1 promise will manifest
- Weak D9 (especially Mrityu Bhaga or enemy sign) = D1 promise may not fully deliver
- Debilitated in D9 = inner resistance or soul-level obstruction to that planet's themes
- D9 mutual aspects = soul-contract level fusion of those planetary themes

## Step 4 — Dasha Timing Analysis

- **Mahadasha lord** — which house does it rule? Which house does it sit in? Is it connected to the question houses?
- **Antardasha lord** — same analysis. Does it support or contradict the Mahadasha theme?
- **Pratyantar** — fine-timing layer for near-term events

**Dasha lord checks:**
- Is the Dasha lord involved in a tight planetary aspect? The aspecting planet co-delivers (or blocks) results
- Is the Dasha lord at a Gandanta, Mrityu Bhaga, or Sandhi degree? Weakens delivery even if well-placed
- Is the Dasha lord combust? Suppressed even if strong otherwise
- Is the Dasha lord functional benefic or malefic for this Lagna?

**Timing indicators:**
- Dasha lord connected to question house = that period is active for the question
- Antardasha lord reinforcing Mahadasha = higher probability of manifestation
- Conflicting Dasha/Antardasha = mixed results, delays, or partial outcomes
- Upcoming Dasha change = flag if it shifts the theme significantly

**Vimshottari Dasha periods (years):**
Sun 6 / Moon 10 / Mars 7 / Rahu 18 / Jupiter 16 / Saturn 19 / Mercury 17 / Ketu 7 / Venus 20
Total cycle = 120 years

---

## Step 5 — Reverse Question Analysis *(Yes/No questions only)*

Frame the opposite of the user's question and repeat Steps 2–4 for that reversed context.

- Does the reverse analysis yield stronger or weaker signatures than the primary?
- If reverse analysis is stronger → answer leans No
- If primary analysis is stronger → answer leans Yes
- If both are equally supported → answer is genuinely unclear, timing-dependent

## Step 6 — Composite Reading with Weighting

Synthesize all layers using this priority order:

| Priority | Factor | Why |
|----------|--------|-----|
| 1 | **Dasha activation** | Promise without activation = no result yet |
| 2 | **D1 house lord dignity + degree status** | Foundational; lord condition = house condition |
| 3 | **Planets in the house** | Direct influence, modified by degree flags |
| 4 | **Mutual planetary aspects (quasi-conjunctions)** | Fused significations behave like conjunctions |
| 5 | **D9 confirmation** | Validates or qualifies D1 promise |
| 6 | **One-way aspects** | Modifying forces; weight by orb + dignity |
| 7 | **Ashtakavarga score** | Quantitative strength check |
| 8 | **Nakshatra nuance + Pada Lord** | Qualitative texture and method of expression |

### Output Structure

**Chart Promise**
What the natal configuration (D1 + D9 + degree flags) structurally promises for this question.

**Dasha Context**
Current Mahadasha → Antardasha → Pratyantar. Is the relevant planet activated? What period supports or delays the outcome?

**Planetary Aspect Fusions**
List tight mutual aspects (within 3°) relevant to the question. Explain the fused lordship themes and net effect.

**Degree Caveats**
Flag any Gandanta (karmic charge), Mrityu Bhaga (suppressed delivery), Sandhi (instability), or Pushkara (empowerment) planets affecting this reading.

**Composite Assessment**
One clear synthesizing paragraph with a directional answer. Do not hedge excessively — commit to what the chart indicates while noting genuine uncertainties.

**Confidence Level:** Strong / Moderate / Unclear
State what drives or limits confidence. Strong = multiple layers agree. Moderate = mixed signals. Unclear = insufficient activation or contradictory D1/D9.
