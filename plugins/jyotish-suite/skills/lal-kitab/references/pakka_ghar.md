# Pakka Ghar, Dignity, and Friendship — Lal Kitab

All dignity in Lal Kitab is by **house number**, never by sign. The tables below are the operative ones — do not use Parashari dignity tables.

---

## 1. Pakka Ghar (Permanent House)

The pakka ghar is the planet's natural seat. A planet sitting in its pakka ghar gives **full results regardless of other afflictions** — this is one of Lal Kitab's strongest single rules.

| Planet  | Pakka Ghar | Significance                      |
|---------|------------|------------------------------------|
| Sun     | 1          | Self, vitality, father (secondary)|
| Moon    | 4          | Mother, home, mind                 |
| Mars    | 3          | Brothers, courage, valour          |
| Mercury | 7          | Spouse, business, communication    |
| Jupiter | 9          | Dharma, father (primary), guru     |
| Venus   | 7          | Wife, comforts, vehicles           |
| Saturn  | 10         | Karma, profession, longevity       |
| Rahu    | 12         | Foreign, hidden, expenses          |
| Ketu    | 6          | Liberation, enemies dissolved      |

**Note on shared 7th house (Mercury + Venus):** When both occupy 7th, results are exceptionally strong for partnership matters but require careful analysis — both giving simultaneously can either compound benefit or create competing significations. Per Farman 14, Vol 3 (1941): when shared, Venus dominates marriage matters and Mercury dominates business.

| Venus   | 6                    |
| Saturn  | 1                    |

A debilitated planet damages the matters of its current house and weakens its own karaka. **Cancellation rules** (Neecha Bhanga) per Lal Kitab differ from Parashari — the most common cancellation is when the lord of the house where debilitation occurs sits in its own pakka ghar. State this only if it applies.

## 5. Friendship Table (Mitra / Shatru / Sama)

Lal Kitab friendship is **fixed and natural** — it does not vary by chart (unlike Parashari's temporary friendship).

| Planet  | Friends            | Enemies           | Neutrals          |
|---------|--------------------|--------------------|--------------------|
| Sun     | Moon, Mars, Jupiter | Saturn, Venus, Rahu | Mercury, Ketu     |
| Moon    | Sun, Mercury        | Rahu, Ketu          | Mars, Jupiter, Venus, Saturn |
| Mars    | Sun, Moon, Jupiter  | Mercury             | Venus, Saturn, Rahu, Ketu |
| Mercury | Sun, Venus, Rahu    | Moon                | Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Ketu |
| Jupiter | Sun, Moon, Mars     | Mercury, Venus      | Saturn, Rahu, Ketu |
| Venus   | Mercury, Saturn, Rahu | Sun, Moon         | Mars, Jupiter, Ketu |
| Saturn  | Mercury, Venus, Rahu  | Sun, Moon, Mars   | Jupiter, Ketu     |
| Rahu    | Mercury, Venus, Saturn | Sun, Moon, Mars  | Jupiter, Ketu     |
| Ketu    | Mars, Venus, Saturn   | Moon              | Sun, Mercury, Jupiter, Rahu |

A planet sitting in a house owned by a friend = friendly placement.
A planet sitting in a house owned by an enemy = enemy placement (results suppressed unless in pakka ghar).

## 6. Blind Houses (Andha Bhava)

Houses **2, 6, 8, 12** are weak/blind by default. Planets here struggle unless:
- They are in their own pakka ghar (e.g., Rahu in 12, Ketu in 6 are pakka ghar placements and *thrive* in these otherwise-blind houses)
- They are aspected by a benefic in good condition

Note the special cases: **Rahu in 12 and Ketu in 6 are NOT weak** — they are in their pakka ghar.

---

## 7. Pakka Ghar Substitution Rule

When a planet is **not** in its pakka ghar, its results are filtered through:
- The **lord of its pakka ghar** (where it should have been)
- Any planet currently **occupying its pakka ghar**

Example: If Sun is in house 7 (debilitated, not in pakka ghar 1), and Mars sits in house 1 — then Sun's results manifest **through Mars's condition**. If Mars is strong, Sun's damage is mitigated. If Mars is weak or sleeping, Sun's damage compounds.

This substitution is the most-used analytical lever in Lal Kitab natal work.

---

## 8. The "Buried" Planet Rule

A planet in the 12th from another planet is said to be **buried (dabba)** by it. Buried planets cannot give results until the burying planet is removed (transit / death of significator-relative / upaay).

Common buried patterns to flag:
- Sun in 12th from Saturn → severe Pitri Rin signature
- Moon in 12th from Rahu → Matri Rin signature
- Venus in 12th from Saturn → marriage delays / Stri Rin
