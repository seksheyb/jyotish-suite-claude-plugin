# Rin (Karmic Debt) Diagnosis — Lal Kitab

The rin system is the diagnostic spine of Lal Kitab. Identifying which debts are active in a chart determines life patterns and which upaay take priority. Run all six checks systematically — never declare "no rin" without having tested all six.

---

## Severity Scale

For each rin found, classify:
- **Mild** — single trigger, no compounding affliction
- **Moderate** — primary trigger + one secondary (aspect / lord weakness)
- **Severe** — primary trigger + two or more compounding factors (sleeping, debilitation, multiple afflictions)

---

## 1. Pitri Rin (Debt to Father / Paternal Ancestors)

**Most common rin in modern charts. Manifests as:** family blocks, paternal property disputes, sons' problems, blocked rise in career, recurring legal/government trouble.

### Triggers (any one is sufficient; multiple = severe)

| # | Configuration | Farman Citation |
|---|---------------|------------------|
| 1 | Sun afflicted by Saturn (conjunction or mutual aspect) | Farman 8, Vol 2 (1940) |
| 2 | Sun afflicted by Rahu (conjunction or aspect) | Farman 8, Vol 2 (1940) |
| 3 | Sun debilitated (in house 7) | Farman 12, Vol 1 (1939) |
| 4 | 9th house empty AND 9th lord (Jupiter) afflicted | Farman 22, Vol 3 (1941) |
| 5 | Saturn or Rahu sits in 9th house | Farman 22, Vol 3 (1941) |
| 6 | Sun in 12th from Saturn (Sun "buried" by Saturn) | Farman 16, Vol 4 (1942) |
| 7 | Both 1st and 9th houses empty AND no aspect on either | Farman 31, Vol 5 (1952) |

### Compounding (escalates to severe)
- Sun also sleeping
- Jupiter also afflicted
- 10th house (karma) also damaged

### Manifestation in life
- Father may have suffered (illness, premature death, separation)
- Native blocked despite hard work
- Sons either delayed, problematic, or absent
- Recurring authority conflicts (boss, government, courts)

### Primary upaay reference
See `upaay_catalog.md` §1 — Pitri Rin remedies (river offerings, wheat to crows, gold ear-piercing for sons).

## Cross-Rin Compounding Rules

When **multiple rins** are present, severity escalates non-linearly:

- 2 rins → each one tier worse than diagnosed individually
- 3+ rins → chart is in **debt-saturation**, prioritize Pitri/Matri (lineage rins) first; others wait until lineage rins are addressed via 6+ months of upaay

When Pitri Rin and Stri Rin are **both severe**, native experiences the classic "blocked-house" pattern: cannot rise in career, cannot stabilize marriage, two domains compete for resources.

---

## When to Declare "No Rin"

State explicitly in output:
- Tested all 6 rin types
- Show which configurations were absent (briefly — one line each)
- Note that absence of rin doesn't mean an effortless life — it means the chart's challenges come from other sources (sleeping planets, debilitations, teva type)
