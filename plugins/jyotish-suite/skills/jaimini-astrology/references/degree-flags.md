## Gandanta Zones

Water→Fire sign junctions. Planets here carry deep karmic knots — their Karaka themes require conscious resolution this lifetime.

| Junction | D1 Range |
|----------|----------|
| Cancer → Leo | 26°40' Cancer – 3°20' Leo |
| Scorpio → Sagittarius | 26°40' Scorpio – 3°20' Sagittarius |
| Pisces → Aries | 26°40' Pisces – 3°20' Aries |

In Jaimini: a Karaka planet at Gandanta = the soul has unresolved karma around those Karaka themes. The karmic knot must be consciously worked through — the themes don't unfold automatically.

## Planetary War (Graha Yuddha)

Two visible planets within 1° of each other in the same sign.

**Eligible planets:** Mars, Mercury, Jupiter, Venus, Saturn (NOT Sun, Moon, Rahu, Ketu)

**Rules:**
- Lower degree planet = **winner** — Karaka themes intact
- Higher degree planet = **defeated** — Karaka themes weakened
- Defeated Karaka's significations are suppressed throughout the chart, regardless of sign dignity
- AK defeated → soul themes obstructed; requires extra conscious effort
- AmK defeated → career significations hampered despite apparent opportunity
- DK defeated → partnership and desire themes weakened

**Close contention (within 2°–5°):** Not a formal war but creates mutual tension. Flag as "in close contention" — note degree gap. Results from both Karakas may be uneasy or competitive.

Flag Planetary War as 🔴 PW. State winner and defeated planet explicitly.

## Degree Flag Priority

When multiple flags apply to the same planet, net effect:

| Combination | Net Reading |
|-------------|-------------|
| Pushkara + strong sign | Exceptionally empowered Karaka |
| Mrityu Bhaga + exalted | Suppressed despite dignity — MB overrides |
| Gandanta + own sign | Karmically charged but capable if consciously worked |
| Sandhi + Pushkara | Instability partially offset by auspicious degree |
| Planetary War (defeated) + Pushkara | Suppression from war; Pushkara helps but doesn't fully override |
| Vargottama + Pushkara | Strongest possible Karaka expression |
| Vargottama + MB | Strong soul-level intent but material delivery blocked |
