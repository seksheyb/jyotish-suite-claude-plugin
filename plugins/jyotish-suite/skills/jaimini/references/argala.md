# Argala Reference

Argala = "intervention" or "bolt" — planets in certain positions from a sign pour their energy into it.

---

## Primary Argala Positions (from any reference sign)

| Position | Type | Signification |
|----------|------|--------------|
| 2nd sign | Primary Argala | Wealth, speech, sustenance, family |
| 4th sign | Primary Argala | Happiness, home, comfort, mother |
| 11th sign | Primary Argala | Gains, fulfilment, friends, desires |
| 5th sign | Secondary Argala | Intellect, children, past karma, merit |

## Virodha Argala (Obstruction)

| Virodha Position | Obstructs |
|-----------------|-----------|
| 12th sign | Obstructs 2nd sign Argala |
| 10th sign | Obstructs 4th sign Argala |
| 3rd sign | Obstructs 11th sign Argala |
| 9th sign | Obstructs 5th sign Argala |

---

## Effectiveness Rule

Argala is effective ONLY if the number of planets in the Argala position exceeds the number of planets in the Virodha position.

- 2 planets in 2nd sign, 1 planet in 12th sign → Argala is effective (2 > 1)
- 1 planet in 2nd sign, 2 planets in 12th sign → Virodha Argala dominates; Argala is obstructed
- Equal numbers → Argala and Virodha cancel; weakened result from both

If the Argala position is empty → no Argala from that position (regardless of Virodha).
If both positions are empty → no Argala, no obstruction; neutral.

---

## Quality of Argala

Not all Argala is equal. The nature of the intervening planets determines quality:

| Planets in Argala Position | Quality |
|---------------------------|---------|
| Natural benefics (Jupiter, Venus, waxing Moon, unafflicted Mercury) | Supportive, growth-oriented Argala |
| Natural malefics (Saturn, Mars, Sun, Rahu, Ketu) | Harsh, demanding, or transformative Argala |
| AK in Argala position | Soul-level sanction — very powerful positive intervention |
| AmK in Argala position on A10 or Karakamsha | Career themes strongly supported |
| DK in Argala position on UL | Partnership/marriage themes strongly supported |
| GK in Argala position | Competitive or health-related intervention |

Malefic Argala is still Argala — it intervenes and influences, but the quality is pressuring rather than nurturing. It can produce results through difficulty.

---

## How to Compute Argala

**For any reference sign (e.g., AL in Cancer):**

1. Identify the reference sign (Cancer)
2. Count 2nd sign from Cancer → Leo. Check for planets in Leo.
3. Count 4th sign from Cancer → Libra. Check for planets in Libra.
4. Count 11th sign from Cancer → Taurus. Check for planets in Taurus.
5. Count 5th sign from Cancer → Scorpio. Check for planets in Scorpio.
6. Check Virodha positions: 12th from Cancer = Gemini, 10th = Aries, 3rd = Virgo, 9th = Pisces.
7. Compare Argala planets vs Virodha planets for each pair.
8. State net effect for each position.
