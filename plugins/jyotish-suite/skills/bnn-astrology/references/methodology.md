## SECTION 2 — BNN Analysis Steps *(Execute for D1 and D9 separately)*

### Step A — Karaka Identification and Sign Field

1. Identify primary Karaka(s) from Section 1A (karaka-tables.md)
2. Locate Karaka's sign in the chart
3. State the sign field (Section 1B)
4. Dignity: exalted / own / friendly / neutral / enemy / debilitated
5. Retrograde flag: if yes → themes internalized, revisited, or delayed; results come via non-linear path
6. **Degree flag scan** (load degree-flags.md — run all checks):
   - Combust? → 🔴 suppressed delivery regardless of dignity
   - Gandanta? → 🔴 karmically charged; results come through difficulty
   - Mrityu Bhaga? → 🔴 weakened delivery; overrides sign dignity
   - Pushkara? → 🟢 empowered; can outperform apparent dignity
   - Sandhi (0°–1° or 29°–30°)? → 🟡 immature or unstable expression
   - Planetary War (within 1° of eligible planet)? → 🔴 PW; state winner/defeated; within 2°–5° → 🟡 CC
   - Apply priority table when multiple flags apply
7. **Aspects received by Karaka's sign** (load aspects.md):
   - Which planets aspect this sign via Graha Drishti? From which sign?
   - For each: signification (1A), relationship to Karaka (1C), orb, degree flags (1D)
   - Tight friendly aspect (within 3°) → powerfully supports Karaka expression
   - Tight enemy aspect (within 3°) → powerfully restricts or pressures
   - **Mutual aspect check:** does the Karaka itself aspect back any aspecting planet within 3°? → quasi-conjunction; significations fuse bidirectionally
8. Composite statement: *"[Planet] as [Karaka signification] operates in the field of [sign domain] — [dignity] — [degree flags] — [aspects: supportive/restrictive/mixed]"*

### Step B — Conjunction Analysis (Same Sign as Karaka)

List all planets in the Karaka's sign.

For each conjunct planet:
- Natural signification (1A) and relationship to Karaka (1C)
- Degree flags (1D): combust → suppressed conjunct modification; MB → unreliable delivery; Pushkara → amplified; Sandhi → inconsistent; Planetary War → state winner/defeated; defeated planet's conjunct influence is weakened
- Net modification after flags:
  - Friendly + no adverse flag → both themes activated together
  - Friendly + adverse flag → theme present but delivery constrained
  - Enemy → tension or interference; flag dominant planet by degree
  - Neutral → context and sign determine interaction
- Three or more planets in same sign → high-concentration zone; read in sequence from primary Karaka outward; apply flags to each
- Retrograde conjunct → signification present but delayed or internalized

### Step C — Flow Positions (2nd and 12th from Karaka)

**2nd from Karaka** = accumulation, future flow, what the Karaka builds toward
**12th from Karaka** = what feeds or precedes the Karaka; expenditure, hidden support, or dissolution

For each position:
1. Identify sign → state field (1B)
2. List **occupying** planets → signification, relationship to Karaka, degree flags; net reading per rules below
3. **Aspects received by this sign** (aspects.md):
   - List all aspecting planets, their signification, relationship to Karaka, orb, degree flags
   - Friendly aspecting planet adds its themes to this position even without occupation
   - Enemy aspecting planet restricts or complicates the position's delivery
   - Tight mutual aspect (within 3°) between aspecting planet and any occupying planet → quasi-conjunction; fused influence
4. Empty position = no occupant AND no aspect → sign lord as tertiary indicator; check sign lord's own degree flags
5. Retrograde in flow position → delayed or internalized flow

**Net reading rules:**
- Friendly occupant or friendly aspect (unflagged) in 2nd → beneficial accumulation in [sign field]
- Friendly + adverse degree flag in 2nd → accumulation promised but blocked at delivery level
- Enemy in 2nd (occupant or aspect) → conflicted or obstructed gain
- Friendly in 12th → nourishes the Karaka from behind
- Enemy in 12th → drains or dissolves the Karaka's themes

### Step D — Trine Support (5th and 9th from Karaka)

**5th from Karaka** = past karma, what the Karaka generates; karmic support
**9th from Karaka** = fortune, dharma, divine grace; what blesses the Karaka

For each position:
1. Identify sign → state field (1B)
2. List **occupying** planets → signification, relationship to Karaka, degree flags (especially critical here: combust or MB planet in 9th = divine support that cannot activate)
3. **Aspects received by this sign** (aspects.md):
   - A planet aspecting the 9th from outside adds its blessing quality to the Karaka's fortune
   - Friendly + tight orb + no adverse flag = strong external karmic backing
   - Degree flags on aspecting planet apply fully
   - Tight mutual aspect → quasi-conjunction; fused trine influence
4. Empty = no occupant AND no aspect → sign lord as tertiary indicator
5. **Strong convergence flag:** planet or aspect in trine mirrors the question topic AND has no adverse degree flags → weight very heavily

**Net reading rules:**
- Benefic + unflagged in trine → strong karmic or divine backing
- Benefic + MB/Combust in trine → support promised but cannot activate
- Malefic in trine → karmic debt or challenge embedded in the Karaka's fortune

### Step E — Growth Positions (3rd and 11th from Karaka)

**3rd from Karaka** = effort, desire, initiative; short-term outcome of striving
**11th from Karaka** = gains, fulfilment, ultimate receipt from the Karaka's themes

For each position:
1. Identify sign → state field (1B)
2. List **occupying** planets → signification, relationship to Karaka, degree flags
3. **Aspects received by this sign** (aspects.md):
   - Aspecting planet contributes gains or effort energy even without occupying
   - Apply relationship quality, orb weighting, degree flags
4. Empty = no occupant AND no aspect → sign lord as tertiary indicator; check degree flags on sign lord

**Net reading rules:**
- Friendly + unflagged in 11th → strong gains in [sign field]
- Friendly + adverse flag in 11th → gains exist but delivery is constrained
- Enemy in 11th → gains come with conflict or delay
- Friendly in 3rd → effort is supported; results from initiative are good
- Enemy in 3rd → effort meets resistance; persistence is required

### Step F — Opposition (7th from Karaka)

**7th from Karaka** = direct counterforce, partnership dimension; external world's response to the Karaka

1. Identify sign → state field (1B)
2. List **occupying** planets → signification, relationship to Karaka, degree flags
3. **Aspects received by this sign** (aspects.md):
   - Any planet aspecting the 7th contributes to the external/partner dimension
   - **Mutual aspect check (critical):** is the Karaka within 3° of aspecting a planet in the 7th? → direct opposition energy; both planets' significations are in active tension or negotiation; read as quasi-conjunction
4. Empty = no occupant AND no aspect → sign lord as tertiary indicator
5. For relationship questions (Venus or Jupiter as Karaka): 7th occupants and aspects show the partner's nature directly; degree flags show whether the partner can deliver

**Net reading rules:**
- Friendly occupant or aspect (unflagged) → supportive external world or partner
- Friendly + adverse flag → support is present but constrained
- Enemy occupant or aspect → friction, opposition, or challenge from external dimension
- Mutual aspect within 3° → highest-intensity opposition/partnership dynamic; fused significations

## SECTION 4 — Dasha Timing Layer

BNN structural readings activate through Vimshottari Dasha.

**Vimshottari periods:** Sun 6 / Moon 10 / Mars 7 / Rahu 18 / Jupiter 16 / Saturn 19 / Mercury 17 / Ketu 7 / Venus 20

**Dasha activation steps:**
1. Current Mahadasha lord — is it the primary Karaka, or naturally friendly to it?
2. Antardasha lord — reinforces or conflicts?
3. **Degree flags on Dasha lord** (degree-flags.md): MB/Gandanta Dasha lord struggles to deliver its period even if friendly Karaka; combust lord is suppressed; Pushkara lord delivers with exceptional ease
4. Karaka's own Dasha = direct activation (modified by its degree flags)
5. Friendly planet's Dasha = secondary activation via ally
6. Enemy planet's Dasha = contested period; delay or conflict in Karaka's domain
7. **Run the Dasha lord through BNN** (Steps A–F): locate its sign, conjunctions, flow, trines, growth positions, aspects — what it promises through BNN is what it delivers in its Dasha period

**Timing output:**
- Current Mahadasha + Antardasha: activates Karaka directly / indirectly / not at all
- Most favorable upcoming Dasha period for this question — which planet, why (what Karaka it is or allies with), when it begins

## SECTION 5 — Mandatory Execution Sequence

Every reading in this exact order. Do not skip or reorder.

**Step 1 — Question Classification**
State primary Karaka(s), reading type, in one line.

**Step 2 — D1 BNN Analysis**
Execute Section 2 Steps A–F for identified Karaka(s) using D1 data.

**Step 3 — D9 BNN Analysis**
Execute Section 3 (full Steps A–F within D9) for same Karaka(s).

**Step 4 — Reverse Question Analysis** *(Yes/No only)*
Reverse the question. Identify Karaka for the reversed context. Repeat Steps A–F for D1 and D9. Compare signature strength to forward analysis to calibrate confidence direction.

**Step 5 — Composite Reading**

Priority order:

| Priority | Factor |
|----------|--------|
| 1 | Dasha activation — Karaka or ally running? Degree flags on Dasha lord? |
| 2 | Karaka's sign, dignity, degree flags in D1 — foundational strength |
| 3 | Aspects received by Karaka's sign — tight friendly/enemy aspects |
| 4 | Conjunctions in Karaka's sign — signification blend, degree flags |
| 5 | 2nd and 12th flow — occupants and aspects, degree flags, net reading |
| 6 | 5th and 9th trine — occupants and aspects, degree flags, convergence flag |
| 7 | D9 confirmation — Vargottama, soul-level endorsement or caveat |
| 8 | 3rd and 11th growth — occupants and aspects, effort and gain quality |
| 9 | 7th opposition — occupants and aspects, external response |
| 10 | Mutual aspects within 3° — quasi-conjunctions; fused Karaka significations |

**Composite output must state:**
- What D1 BNN structurally indicates (Karaka sign field + flow + trine)
- What D9 confirms or modifies
- Whether D1 and D9 converge or diverge — practical meaning of any divergence
- Which Dasha period activates or blocks the outcome
- **Confidence: Strong / Moderate / Unclear** — based on convergence across D1, D9, trine, Dasha, and aspect quality
