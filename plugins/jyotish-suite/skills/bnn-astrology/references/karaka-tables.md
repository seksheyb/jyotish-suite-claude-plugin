## 1C — Natural Planet Relationships

Determines whether a planet in a flow, trine, growth, or opposition position supports or obstructs the Karaka.

| Planet | Friends | Neutrals | Enemies |
|--------|---------|----------|---------|
| Sun | Moon, Mars, Jupiter | Mercury | Venus, Saturn, Rahu, Ketu |
| Moon | Sun, Mercury | Mars, Jupiter, Venus, Saturn | Rahu, Ketu |
| Mars | Sun, Moon, Jupiter | Venus, Saturn, Ketu | Mercury, Rahu |
| Mercury | Sun, Venus, Rahu | Mars, Jupiter, Saturn | Moon, Ketu |
| Jupiter | Sun, Moon, Mars | Saturn | Mercury, Venus, Rahu, Ketu |
| Venus | Mercury, Saturn, Rahu | Mars, Jupiter | Sun, Moon, Ketu |
| Saturn | Mercury, Venus, Rahu | Jupiter | Sun, Moon, Mars, Ketu |
| Rahu | Mercury, Venus, Saturn | Mars, Jupiter | Sun, Moon, Ketu |
| Ketu | Mars, Venus | Mercury, Saturn | Sun, Moon, Jupiter, Rahu |

**Application:**
- Friendly planet → supportive, enhancing influence on Karaka themes
- Enemy planet → conflicted, obstructing, or complicating influence
- Neutral → mixed; read by sign field and context
- Retrograde friendly → supportive but delayed or internalized
- Retrograde enemy → persistent or recurring obstruction
